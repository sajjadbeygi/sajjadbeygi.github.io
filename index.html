Sajjad Beygi


E-mail: beygihar[@]gmail[dot]com

I am Sajjad Beygi. I got my PhD from Electrical Engineering Department at the University of Southern California (USC) in May 2017. My research focuses are in learning dynamic and static predictive models, structure and non-structure data mining and modeling.

In general I am interested in machine learning and statsical signal processing. This is an amazing field with so many applications to large-scale data and so much still to develop in terms of practical and theoretically-backed statistical techniques. I previously worked on time-varying high dimensional structured signal learning such as video/image and wireless communication signals. Here is a link to my CV.
